Hi, I'm Zeyi Liao.

I'm a third-year PhD student fortunately advised by Prof. Huan Sun at OSU. Before that, I worked with Prof. Xiang Ren during my Master study at USC and obtained my Bachelor's degree from BJTU.

I'm broadly interested in AI with a focus on NLP and multimodal AI. My career goal is to build helpful yet robust AI system to make life easier.

My current research preference (subject to change):

  • Critically examining and challenging so-called "true claims" in AI through retrospective analysis
  • Delving beyond superficial observations to uncover the underlying mechanisms and principles
